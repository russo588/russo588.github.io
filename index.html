About Me

I am currently an Assistant Professor at Farmingdale State College SUNY. I was previously a Visiting Assistant Professor at the University of Connecticut. I earned my Ph. D. at the University of Florida in 2016 working with Dr. Scott McCullough on modeling tuples of 3-isometries as sub-Jordan operators and the applications of these models to Schrödinger operators.

Mathematical interests:

  • functional analysis
  • operator theory
